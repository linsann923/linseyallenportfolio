About Me

With a background in marriage and family therapy, combined with my enthusiasm for creativity and problem-solving, I am a valuable addition to any team dedicated to making a meaningful impact. My Story

My original career trajectory was focused on preventing domestic violence, stalking, and sexual assault. While that is still my passion, I learned through hands on experience that immediate contact with such high intensity situations was too difficult for me.

When I found UX/UI design, I knew I had found the right career. With my background in marriage and family therapy, and my love of design, this field provided the perfect opportunity to learn incredible skills and use them for good.

My journey has equipped me with a diverse skill set. I am incredibly empathetic and highly logical. I am qualified to speak and design on users' behalf.
